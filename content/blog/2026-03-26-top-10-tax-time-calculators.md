## FAQ

### Which tax calculator is best for beginners?
**TI-30XS MultiView** or **HP 10C** offer the best balance of simplicity and functionality for beginners.

### Are HP financial calculators worth the extra cost?
Absolutely for serious tax planning. The **HP 12C** and **HP 15C** are industry standards with proven reliability.

### Can I use scientific calculators for tax work?
Yes, models like **TI-36X Pro** and **Casio fx-991EX** offer advanced scientific functions suitable for tax calculations.

### What's the difference between RPN and algebraic mode?
**RPN** (Reverse Polish Notation) uses a stack-based approach preferred by advanced users. **Algebraic mode** (standard entry) is more intuitive for beginners.

### Are physical calculators better than online tools for taxes?
For tax planning and documentation, physical calculators offer:
- Reliability without internet
- Tactile feedback for accuracy
- Portable for on-the-go calculations
- No registration or account requirements
