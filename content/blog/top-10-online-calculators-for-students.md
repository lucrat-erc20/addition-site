## FAQ

### At what age should kids start using calculators?

Most experts suggest waiting until kids understand basic arithmetic (grades 4-5) before introducing calculators as learning tools.

### Are these calculators exam-approved?

Most of the calculators mentioned are approved for major standardized tests. Always check your specific exam requirements.

### Which calculator is best for high school?

TI-84 Plus CE is the most popular choice for high school students due to widespread adoption and exam approval.

### Can I use these calculators on smartphones?

No, these are physical handheld calculators designed for traditional use. They're not apps or digital calculators.

### How long do these calculators last?

With proper care, quality calculators like HP and TI models can last through high school and into college.
